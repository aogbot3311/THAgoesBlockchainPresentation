Pain Points

What goes wrong

  • ✕ Time-consuming manual processes ( ~8 hours/month per SME)
  • ✕ Delayed settlement — weeks to months after sale
  • ✕ Fraud opportunities through missing trader schemes
  • ✕ Complex refund procedures for input VAT
  • ✕ Difficult audits without a tamper-proof audit trail

Technology Choice

Why Blockchain?

Traditional Database
✕ Editable & deletable records
✕ Single point of failure
✕ Requires trust in one party
✕ Opaque to auditors
Blockchain
✓ Immutable > records cannot be altered
✓ Traceable > full transaction history
✓ Auditable > transparent to regulators
✓ Transparent > no hidden manipulation
✓ Decentralised > no single point of trust
